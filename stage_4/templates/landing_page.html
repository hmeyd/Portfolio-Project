Useful Articles

The SIRET number identifies each branch. The SIREN is common to all branches of a company. The APE code reflects the main business activity.

The BODACC publishes official announcements of a company: creation, sales, legal liquidations, etc.

The statutes define the activity, capital distribution, executive powers, and internal rules of the company.

Define the activity, choose a legal form, draft statutes, deposit the capital, register with the RCS, and publish a notice of incorporation.
